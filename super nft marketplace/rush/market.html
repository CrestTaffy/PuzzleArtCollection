Freaquently Asked
Question

Let's Talk about it.

What is the My Pocket Token Foundation?

The My Pocket TokenFoundation is a blockchain development foundation, with a mission to make the blockchain better.

What is the Puzzle Art Collection?

The Puzzle Art Collection is a collection of physical artwork tokenized as NFTs.

Is there a White Paper?

No, the Foundation does not use a White Paper. You may learn more or read a down to earth copy of what is going on in the Official Pocket Paper.

Is MetaMask required?

Yes, MetaMask is required to use any of the NFT Marketplaces.

Can I sell my NFTs?

Yes, you may sell, swap, or trade your NFTs on OpenSea Polygon or migrate to any platform you choose.

What is the price of the NFTs?

Most NFTs listed are in ICO stage and do not have a starting price. The prices listed are only suggested starting prices. Users aer free to bid higher or lower if needed.
